Food Demand Forecasting

Forcasting food demand daily and weekly has become an essential step for food delivery services for better inventory management and be ready with the future chanlenges. Poor inventory management in the warehouse can lead to more risk of wastage or on other hand can lead to out-of-stocks - and push customers to seek solutions from your competitors. he replenishment of majority of raw materials is done on weekly basis and since the raw material is perishable, the procurement planning is of utmost importance.

The main aim of this project is to create an appropriate machine learning model to forecast then number of orders to gather raw materials for next 10 weeks.
